Tuition fees at Brunel University London

Students who are citizens of the EU/EEA:

The average tuition fees for students from the EU/EEA are 8,400 GBP per year, ranging from 6,300 GBP to 8,900 GBP.

Students who are citizens from outside the EU/EEA:

The average tuition fees for students from outside the EU/EEA are 15,700 GBP per year, ranging from 10,800 GBP to 18,300 GBP.

Tuition fees may vary for each study programme.
