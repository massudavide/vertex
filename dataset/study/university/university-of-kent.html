The University is one of the country’s leading academic institutions producing world-class research, rated internationally excellent and leading the way in many fields of study.

We are a forward-thinking research institution, committed to the transformative power of education and research and to the development and support of our students and staff.

Additional information

Application deadlines

The University of Kent still accepts late applications from EU students looking to start their studies in autumn 2020.

English requirements

When you apply to the University of Kent, you will need to prove your English proficiency.

  • This university accepts IELTS.
  • This university accepts TOEFL iBT. The TOEFL institution code (DI Code) for the University of Kent is: 0826.
  • This university accepts Cambridge English certificates (CAE/CPE).
  • This university accepts the Pearson Test of English (PTE).
