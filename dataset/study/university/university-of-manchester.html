Tuition fees at the University of Manchester

Students who are citizens of the EU/EEA:

The average tuition fees for students from the EU/EEA are 10,700 GBP per year, ranging from 4,200 GBP to 46,800 GBP.

Students who are citizens from outside the EU/EEA:

The average tuition fees for students from outside the EU/EEA are 19,700 GBP per year, ranging from 12,700 GBP to 46,800 GBP.

Tuition fees may vary for each study programme.
