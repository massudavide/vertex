The Week Ahead

Jets ready to take flight

by Michael Finewax
January 15, 2021, 11:44 am ET
Updated On: January 15, 2021, 11:44 am ET
Print

Well, we are two nights into the 2020-21 schedule and there has been a postponement, although we all knew about that last Friday.

You can really tell there were only 10 days of practice for most teams as well as no pre-season games but by the time the end of Week 2, teams should be a lot better, as well as the pace of the game.

Happy Birthday to my brother Larry!

Lets get to it!

WEEK OF JANUARY 18-24

5 Games Winnipeg

4 Games Anaheim, Arizona, Buffalo, Carolina, Colorado, Columbus, Detroit, Edmonton, Los Angeles, Minnesota, Montreal, Nashville, Philadelphia, San Jose, St. Louis, Toronto, Vancouver, Vegas

3 Games Boston, Chicago, Dallas, Florida, New Jersey, New York Islanders, New York Rangers, Ottawa, Pittsburgh, Tampa Bay, Washington

2 Games - Calgary

Anaheim, Arizona and St. Louis play four games at home.

Buffalo, Colorado, Montreal, San Jose and Vegas play four games on the road.

Pittsburgh, Toronto and Vancouver play three games at home.

Edmonton and Winnipeg play three games on the road.
