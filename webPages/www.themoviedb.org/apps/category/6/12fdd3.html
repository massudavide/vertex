• Discover Popular, In Theaters, Genres, Coming Soon and Top Rated movies.

• Get more details about your movies (ratings, trailer, runtime, overview, genres, date, cast, crew ...)

• Get your statistics about your "Watched" movies

Movie Explorer

By Betamagic

Movie Explorer is a cinema movie file manager for macOS that scans all your local and network drives for MKV, M4V, MP4 and other movie files and saves them in a catalog for off line browsing and searching.
